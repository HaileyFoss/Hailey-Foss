Results & Impact

User Feedback

While formal user testing wasn't conducted prior to development, the website has received significant positive feedback:

  • New patients cited the website as their primary reason for choosing AOPC over closer competitors
  • Users particularly appreciated the header video showcasing the clinic's unique facilities
  • The highlight of Alberta's only gait room and the learning centre resonated with patients
  • Improved visibility of clinic amenities and educational resources received positive recognition
